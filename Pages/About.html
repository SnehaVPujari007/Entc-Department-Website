Government College of Engineering Yavatmal was established by Government of Maharashtra in 2018 through upgradation of old Government Polytechnic with an objective of providing Technical education to the students from rural and educationally backward area. The institute is located in the educational area of the city and is spread over the lush green campus of 40.19 acres.

Our College

The institute offers undergraduate engineering education (B.Tech.) in five disciplines Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, Electronics and Telecommunication and Computer Engineering with an intake of 60 each. The total numbers of students studying in the institute are more than 1300.

The institute is affiliated to the Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University,Lonere Dist. Raigad. The students admitted in the institute are meritorious and most of the students are from rural areas of Yavatmal and neighbouring districts. Being Government, the institute is charging least fees to students as compared to other engineering institutes in the district.
